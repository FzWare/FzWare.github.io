FzRemote Quick Start

Mon 15 July 2019

  1. Insert 2xAAA batteries.

  2. Run FzRemote app, Info tab should show Connected.

  3. Goto Search Tab, select device type and brand name, click "Find My Remote".

  4. Point remote control to your device, click power button one by one until your device response, then click the i button.
    If first few remote doesn't work, Click fast forward button on the right top corner for auto search.

  5. Click each function to find out whether it works for your device, click star button to mark it as favorite function, click upload button to assign function to a phisical remote button.

  6. Goto Favorite Tab, now you can control your device by click favorite function.

  7. Goto Dashboard Tab, configure virtual remote and activity.
